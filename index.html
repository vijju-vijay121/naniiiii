RPF RAILWAY PROTECTION FORCE,RECRUITMENT 2024,DETAILS

Notification released from RPF(railway protection force) 2024,eligible candidates can be able to apply through the official RPF website.Recruitment for constables and Sub-Inspectors.No of vacancies to be filled shall be decided by ministry of railways from time to time.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS WILL BE HELD IN THREE PHASES

1.Computer Based Test(CBT)
2.PET and PMT
3.Document Verification
